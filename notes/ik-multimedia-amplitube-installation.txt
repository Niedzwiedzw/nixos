AMP modeller installation:
1. go to https://www.ikmultimedia.com/userarea/my-products/
2. download the windows release into ~/.vst
3. run: export WINEPREFIX="$(pwd)/pfx"
4. run: wine ./ProductManager_1_1_9/Install\ IK\ Product\ Manager\ \(v1.1.9\).exe
5. click 'download' for amplitube
6. install to Z:\home\niedzwiedz\.vst\ik-multimedia-stuff\amplitube-5
 - plugins to Z:\home\niedzwiedz\.vst\ik-multimedia-stuff\amplitube-5--plugins
 - vst3    to Z:\home\niedzwiedz\.vst\ik-multimedia-stuff\amplitube-5--vst3
 - vst3    to Z:\home\niedzwiedz\.vst\ik-multimedia-stuff\amplitube-5--plug-ins
7. run: yabridgectl --add ~/.vst
8. yabridgectl sync
